About Bin Day Brewing

Bin Day Brewing began as a lockdown homebrew experiment between Robbie Knox and Mike Millar It grew into a quirky, small-batch brewery that was well receieved by fans.

Although the brewery has closed, its charm and the community spirit it stood for continue to inspire. This site stands as a fan tribute. I was too young to try any of the beers- not morally, I just didn't like beer- so I am particularly disappointed that I'll never get to try it.

Bin Day Brewery Co. shut down because Robbie and Mike became very busy. The project began in lockdown, when we all had nothing to do, but after lockdown ended it became clear that nor Robbie nor Mike had enough time to put as much work into the brewery as they would've liked. So, the difficult decision was made to put BDB on ice for the forseeable future.
